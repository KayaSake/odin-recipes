Salad

Spinich is a healthy and tasty base for any salad.

Ingredients

  • Spinich
  • Chevre
  • Bell Peppers
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • balsamic vinegar
  • Oil
  1. Wash all produce
  2. Chop peppers onions, and save extra for later salads
  3. Mix
  4. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and olive oil
