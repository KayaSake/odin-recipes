Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious homemade chocolate chip cookies - perfect for the holidays!

ingredients

  • granulated sugar (1 cup)
  • packed brown sugar (1 cup
  • softened unsalted butter (1 cup)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon sald
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chip cookies
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. cream together granulated sugar, bown sugar and butter
  3. beat in eggs, one at a time
  4. add vanilla extract
  5. add salt
  6. dissolve baking soda in hot water and mix into batter
  7. add chocolate chips and walnuts
  8. portion 2 oz balls onto baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes, or until edges start to brown.
